Our Transparent Engagement Approach

Every security engagement is executed through a clearly defined, authorization-driven, and outcome-focused methodology. Our robust approach ensures quality Security assessments that are not only comprehensive but also actionable and aligned with your business goals.

01

Pre-Engagement Phase

This phase ensures the engagement is legally authorized, well-scoped, and aligned with business priorities before any testing activity begins.

  • Detailed scope definition (applications, APIs, IP ranges, environments)
  • Rules of engagement and testing boundaries
  • Formal authorization and NDA execution
  • Timeline finalization and stakeholder alignment
  • Understanding application architecture and business risk
02

Engagement Phase

The assessment is conducted using a blend of manual testing and controlled automation, focusing on real-world exploitability and business impact.

  • Manual vulnerability discovery and exploitation validation
  • Automated scans to ensure comprehensive coverage
  • Correlation and elimination of false positives
  • Severity classification based on impact and likelihood
  • High-quality reporting in PDF and Excel formats
03

Post-Engagement Phase

Post-assessment support ensures findings are clearly understood, remediated correctly, and independently validated.

  • Detailed report walkthrough with technical and business teams
  • Actionable remediation guidance and best practices
  • Developer clarification and Q&A sessions
  • Remediation retesting to validate fixes
  • Final confirmation of vulnerability closure and risk reduction
